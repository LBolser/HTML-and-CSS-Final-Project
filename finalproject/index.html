   These photos are images from some of my favorite places in Washington state. I have included links above to allow you to explore the areas yourself, if you ever get the chance to do so. I fell in love with everything about Seattle. From the flora and fauna to the weather, from Pike Place to other attractions down the Mercer St exit, such as the EMP building, the Space Needle, and of course Chihuley Garden and Glass. Everything there seems magical in it's own way.

At the top of the page is a navigation bar that will take you to the more information for each of the places in Washington I have listed here.

To the side is a menu with various social media and similar accounts about me. Enjoy!
